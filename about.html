About Akontombra Senior High School

History

BRIEF HISTORY OF THE SCHOOL: The school was established and absorbed by the Ghana Education Service in January, 1994 through the initiative of the chiefs and people of Akontombra in the then Sefwi Wiawso District who unanimously realized the dynamics of secondary school education on the socio-economic development of individuals and the society at large.

Our Vision

Our Vision of the school: To identify academic potentials within the Akontombra community and elsewhere, and to encourage and help develop such talents under mutually cheap and affordable partnership terms.

Our Mission

The mission of the school: To provide access for cheap and affordable secondary school education to a marginalized and deprived rural pupils who would have otherwise been deprived of secondary school education due to ignorance and high level of poverty.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold honesty and strong moral principles in all our endeavors.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in academics and personal development.
  • Community Engagement: We foster a supportive and inclusive environment that encourages collaboration and service.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and continuous improvement in teaching and learning.
